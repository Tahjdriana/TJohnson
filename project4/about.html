About Us

Brilliantly Bookish is a site designed to help young adult readers find new books. We also encourage you to shop at independent book stores in the DC, Maryland, and Virginia area by providing a comprehensive list of the stores in those areas. There is also a contact form for you to suggest your own recommendations of books you think should be added to the website.
