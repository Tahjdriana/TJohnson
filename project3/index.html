About the Band

Dark Dark Dark is an American folk band that originated in Minneapolis, Minnesota and formed in 2006.
It's members include:

  • Nona Marie Envie
  • Marshall LaCount
  • Walt McClements
  • Mark Trecka
  • Adam Wozniak
  • Jonathan Kaiser
  • Todd Chandler
  • Brett Bullion
